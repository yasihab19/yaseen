Tactical Squad Games
Tactical Squad Games are a popular type of squad-based video game where players engage in strategic combat scenarios. These games often require careful planning, coordination, and execution of tactics to overcome challenges.

Examples of Tactical Squad Games

  1. Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege
  2. Squad
  3. Arma 3
  4. Ghost Recon Breakpoint
  5. Insurgency: Sandstorm
